This document explains the process of uploading a record.

To initiate the process, click on the "Quick Create" plus menu and Click on New upload.

After clicking on "New Upload," you will be directed to the Upload page.

On the Upload page, click on "Select a Community.

After clicking on "Select a Community," a new modal will appear. Click on the desired community.

You will see your selected community on the Upload page.

To upload your files, click on the "Upload" button. If your record is metadata only, you can select the "Metadata-only record" checkbox.

Choose the resource type for your record.

Click on add creator.

If you cannot find your ORCID, you have the option to add your name manually.

Select the visibility settings for your record.

Click on Preview

You can preview your record before submitting it. If you need to make changes, click "Back" to edit.

After previewing and making any necessary changes, click on "Submit for Review."

By submitting your record, you agree to our Terms of Service.

Click on "Submit Review."

During the record approval process, you have the option to chat with the community manager/curator.

After submitting your record, you will still be able to view and edit it.
