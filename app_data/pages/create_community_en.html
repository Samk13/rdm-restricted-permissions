Creating a New Community in Invenio-RDM

Invenio-RDM provides a feature for creating communities, which are collections of related records. Communities allow for a grouped presentation and curation of records. In this guide, we'll walk you through the steps to create a new community.

Prerequisites

  • Admin or community creation privileges.

Steps to Create a New Community

  1. Login to your Invenio-RDM instance

    Navigate to the login page and enter your credentials. Ensure you have the required permissions to create communities.

  2. Go to the Communities Section

    From the main dashboard, click on the 'Communities' tab in the navigation menu.

  3. Create a New Community

    Click on the 'Create New Community' button. This will take you to a form where you can input details about your community.

    • Community name: Provide a unique name for your community.
    • Identifier: This is your community's unique identifier. You will be able to access your community through the URL: https://rdm-site.se/communities/
    • Community visibility:rather your created community is publicly accessible and shows up in search results or restricted to users with access if you select Restricted.

Next Steps

After creating your community, consider the following:

  • Add records to your community.
  • Invite other users to join and contribute.
  • Customize the community's look and feel, if desired.
